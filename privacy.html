Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

PlatePlanr helps you plan meals, log food, and build grocery lists. This policy describes what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Account info — email and display name if you create an account (Firebase Authentication).
  • App data — meal plans, recipes, grocery lists, calorie logs, and settings you save. Stored locally on your device and, if you subscribe to Pro, synced to Firebase Firestore.
  • Payment info — processed by Stripe; we do not store card numbers.
  • Photos — meal photos you choose to log stay on your device unless you use cloud sync (Pro).

How we use data

To provide the app, sync across devices (Pro), process subscriptions, and improve the service. We do not sell your personal data.

Third parties

We use Firebase (Google), Stripe, and optional APIs (e.g. Open Food Facts for barcode lookup). Each has its own privacy policy.

Contact

Questions: contact the site operator at the email listed on your Netlify site or GitHub repo.
